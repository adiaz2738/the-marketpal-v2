What are the stock analysis research papers all about?

Research papers don't have a specific publishing date. Whenever I find a stock I think is interesting and worth looking into, I'll write a multi-page research paper about the stock, why I'm looking into it, why I'm considering starting a position in it, why I already started a position in it, or why I'm not going to invest in it, if it's a stock I was considering in the past, or just wanted to give my opinion on it. These can be helpful to those of you who still don't know how to research companies, what to look for, and what to avoid, if you're just looking for a starting point on your own research, or if you're just curious about it. My research criteria follows a checklist of things I look for and I'll have a check mark on the aspects of the business are favorable and I'll leave the ones that could be improved or are unfavorable, blank. Valuing companies and figuring out whether or not they're a good investment, doesn't invlove anything too fancy, you do need to understand accounting, at least on a very basic level. I personally am no accountant either, but I am always trying to expand my knowledge on the topic. There is no need to understand overly complicated algibraic formulas, calculus, or any of that fancy stuff, just basic adding, subtraction, multiplication, and division, and you should be able to follow along just fine. I do try to make it as easy as possible to follow along and understand the information I'm covering, however, if there's something you do not understand, I have internal resources I've created myself, as well as some external sources from other websites to make it as easy as possible for you so you don't have to go on a journey all over the internet to find it. You can also email me with any questions you have about the research, as well as any feedback or suggestions. Feel free to leave any suggestions for stocks you'd like to see in a research paper in the future. If you are a Patron, and you do understand how to research companies, you can send me your own research paper on a stock you like, and I'll post it on here with your information, e.g. first name, last name, any contact info you want to provide, or link to your social media.
