What are the stock analysis research papers all about?

Every other week on Sundays, I'll release a new stock analysis research paper. In these research papers I'll go over the most important information about a stock that any investor should be looking for when researching a company to invest in. This is a process that takes hours and can get quite boring and tedious for a lot of people, with having to read 100+ page reports, filings, listen to earnings calls, any much more additional research. These analysis papers take all of that information and give you the important fundamental aspects of the business that you should know and understand so that you can go off and continue doing additional research on your own.
